"Can Love Happen Twice?"

-Ravinder Singh.

Can Love Happen Twice is the 2011 sequel to Ravinder Singh’s debut hit, I Too Had a Love Story. It asks if love can start again after loss. This review looks at its themes of healing, romance, and mental health through Ravin’s story. Ravin’s depression makes his journey to love again tough. Ravinder Singh, an MBA from the Indian School of Business, wrote this novel. It’s set in Brussels and features a radio drama on 93.5 RED FM. The story questions if love can come back, showing Ravin and Simar’s different views. It’s available on Flipkart for ₹84 and is a follow-up to a huge hit. Yet, it gets mixed reviews.
