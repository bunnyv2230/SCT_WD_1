Will you still love me?

-Ravinder Singh.

The plot is concerned with how love blossoms between Rajveer, a reckless boy from Punjab, and Lavanya, an easygoing Meghalayan girl of Assamese origin. The narrative is fast-paced and intriguing. It catches the reader’s attention right from the moment destiny brings the two together in an Indigo flight. From the initial charms of romantic love, the story undergoes a change to a point where the author has exposed the hypocritical attitude of most mainland Indians to their Northeastern compatriots (of whose existence the former is blissfully unaware). The fact that this issue hasn’t been addressed enough in our literature is what makes this a must-read.
